My Hobbies

  • Writing - My number one hobby is also a form of part-time job. I enjoy writing stories and releasing them as novels which then earn me a little money. Whether other people also enjoy them is another matter.
  • Gym - I'm extremely physically active, lifting and running four times a week, always pushing myself in an effort to stay young.
  • Motorcycles - One of my big passions in life is motorcycles. I've owned 13 bikes to date, and only fallen off a couple of them.
  • Pipe Smoking - Not many people smoke traditional pipes these days. I, however, am one of them, and a member of several pipe-smoking clubs. Fun fact, I once came 8th in the British Pipe Smoking Championships. Yes, there is such a thing.
  • Whiskey - does drinking single-malt whiskey count as a hobby? If not, it should.
